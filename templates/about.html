About

The world is full of vibrant colors, but unfortunately, not everyone experiences them in the same way. Colorblindness affects millions of people worldwide, making it challenging to distinguish between certain colors, particularly red and green.

This differentiator aims to bridge the gap between colorblind individuals and the colorful world around them. It empowers people with color vision deficiencies to differentiate between red and green hues with ease and confidence.

Contained in a user-friendly interface, individuals can explore various tools and resources designed to enhance their color perception. Whether you're a designer, artist, student, or simply someone who wants to experience the full spectrum of colors, this differentiator is here to support you.

Created with the resources of Teachable Machine, W3Schools, and p5.js for Crossroads CAS Computer Science.
